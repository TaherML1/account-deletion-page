Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from our game, please follow the steps below. We have included screenshots to guide you through the process.

Steps to Delete Your Account In-App:

  1. Open the App:
    Launch the game on your device.
  2. Navigate to Account Settings:
    Go to the main menu and select the "Settings" option.
  3. Select "Setting Button":
    In the settings menu, find and select the "Delete Account" option.
  4. Confirm Deletion:
    Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the deletion of your account.

Email Request for Account Deletion:

If you prefer to request account deletion via email, please send an email to support@yourwebsite.com with your account details and a request for account deletion.

Data Deletion Policy:

All personal data, including your username, game progress, and any in-app purchases, will be permanently deleted from our servers. We retain minimal data required for compliance with legal obligations for up to 30 days.

For any questions or assistance, please contact our support team at support@yourwebsite.com.
