Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated data from our game, please follow the instructions below.

⚠️ Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. This will remove all your game progress, purchases, and personal data from our systems.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Game App
    Launch the game on your device and make sure you're logged in to your account.
  2. Access Account Settings
    Navigate to your profile section and tap on the settings icon in the top right corner.
  3. Select Delete Account
    Scroll down to find the "Delete Account" option and tap on it.
  4. Confirm Deletion
    Follow the prompts to confirm you want to permanently delete your account.

What Happens Next?

After you initiate deletion, we will permanently remove all your personal data from our servers within 30 days. Some anonymized gameplay data may be retained for analytical purposes as outlined in our Privacy Policy.
